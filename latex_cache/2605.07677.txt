Existing benchmarks span entity-level dialogue , topic threading , goal-driven multi-domain recommendation , and KG-grounded paths ; recent systems add knowledge fusion, persona/contextual adaptation, reasoning, multimodal interaction, language-modelled item interaction, and synthetic CRS dialogue~.
