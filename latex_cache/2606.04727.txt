Early efforts adapt language model architectures for recommendation tasks, demonstrating improvements over conventional collaborative filtering methods~. With the emergence of powerful pre-trained LLMs~, subsequent research explores their effectiveness for zero-shot~ and few-shot~ recommendation without task-specific training.
