### [1] [Calibrated Fusion for Heterogeneous Graph-Vector Retrieval in Multi-Hop QA](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.28886)
*Andre Bacellar*

Main category: cs.IR

TL;DR: 本文提出ThermoFusion方法，通过百分位秩归一化将向量检索和基于图的检索的异构分数映射到统一尺度，实现异构检索融合，在多跳问答任务中显著提升最后一跳检索性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 多跳问答需要检索形成推理链的多个证据段落，其中后跳段落通常与原始查询弱对齐。密集检索在词汇和语义匹配方面表现良好，但会错过那些相关性通过图结构而非直接文本相似性传递的桥接段落。基于图的检索通过连接性和扩散（如个性化PageRank）提供补充信号。然而，密集检索和图检索的分数分布不同（余弦相似度呈窄高斯分布，PPR分数呈幂律分布），导致直接融合效果不佳，而仅基于排名的融合则丢弃了重要的幅度信息。

Method: 提出ThermoFusion方法，核心是异构分数校准：1) 使用百分位秩归一化将每个检索器的分数映射到统一的单位尺度；2) 将归一化的百分位秩转换为能量值；3) 通过温度参数进行Boltzmann加权，温度根据平均能量自适应设置；4) 使用混合参数α和共识提升参数β进行融合。该方法保留了分数分布信息，使异构分数具有可比性。

Result: 在MuSiQue和2WikiMultiHopQA基准测试的HippoRAG2风格设置下，ThermoFusion显著提升最后一跳检索性能：在MuSiQue上，R@5从75.1%提升到77.8%（p=0.039）；在2WikiMultiHopQA上，R@5从51.7%提升到54.0%（p=0.022）。消融实验表明，百分位秩归一化比最小-最大归一化更具鲁棒性，而Boltzmann加权与线性融合在归一化后效果相当。

Conclusion: 异构分数校准是图-向量检索融合的核心问题。百分位秩归一化使异构分数具有可比性，比融合算子的具体选择更为重要。在MuSiQue和2WikiMultiHopQA基准测试中，校准后的融合显著优于仅向量检索和基于排名的融合。图质量约束（实体消歧、池截断和数据库隔离）与融合公式本身同等重要。

Abstract: Graph-augmented retrieval combines dense similarity with graph-based relevance signals such as Personalized PageRank (PPR), but these scores have different distributions and are not directly comparable. We study this as a score calibration problem for heterogeneous retrieval fusion in multi-hop question answering. Our method, PhaseGraph, maps vector and graph scores to a common unit-free scale using percentile-rank normalization (PIT) before fusion, enabling stable combination without discarding magnitude information.
  Across MuSiQue and 2WikiMultiHopQA, calibrated fusion improves held-out last-hop retrieval on HippoRAG2-style benchmarks: LastHop@5 increases from 75.1% to 76.5% on MuSiQue (8W/1L, p=0.039) and from 51.7% to 53.6% on 2WikiMultiHopQA (11W/2L, p=0.023), both on independent held-out test splits. A theory-driven ablation shows that percentile-based calibration is directionally more robust than min-max normalization on both tune and test splits (1W/6L, p=0.125), while Boltzmann weighting performs comparably to linear fusion after calibration (0W/3L, p=0.25). These results suggest that score commensuration is a robust design choice, and the exact post-calibration operator appears to matter less on these benchmarks.

### [2] [Aligning Multimodal Sequential Recommendations via Robust Direct Preference Optimization with Sparse MoE](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.29259)
*Hejin Huang,Jusheng Zhang,Kaitong Cai,Jian Wang,Rong Pan*

Main category: cs.IR

TL;DR: 本文提出RoDPO（Robust Direct Preference Optimization）框架，针对多模态序列推荐中的隐式反馈问题，通过随机Top-K采样策略解决DPO在推荐系统中因假阴性导致的性能下降问题，显著提升了推荐排名性能。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 直接偏好优化（DPO）在NLP领域取得了显著成功，但在推荐系统中的应用面临独特挑战。推荐系统中的隐式反馈存在"假阴性困境"：未点击的物品不一定是用户不喜欢的，可能只是未被观察到。标准的DPO策略（如硬负采样）会错误地将未观察到的正样本作为负样本进行惩罚，引入错误的偏好梯度，导致排名空间扭曲和性能下降。现有研究很少系统研究DPO在隐式反馈下的行为，特别是负采样策略与DPO训练的相互作用。

Method: RoDPO框架包含三个核心组件：1）随机Top-K采样策略：从模型预测的前K个候选池（排除正样本）中均匀采样负样本，平衡梯度强度和假阴性鲁棒性；2）稀疏混合专家编码器：可选组件，通过稀疏MoE层扩展模型容量以处理细粒度偏好冲突；3）两阶段训练协议：第一阶段使用标准交叉熵损失预热模型，第二阶段冻结参考策略并优化DPO目标。具体地，DPO损失函数基于对数差：L_DPO = -log σ(β[Δs_θ - Δs_ref])，其中Δs = s(y_w) - s(y_l)。

Result: 在三个Amazon基准数据集（Beauty、Toys and Games、Sports and Outdoors）上的实验表明：1）RoDPO在所有指标上均优于最先进的多模态基线方法，相比最强基线HM4SR，在NDCG@5上提升达7.67%；2）随机Top-K采样策略显著优于随机采样和硬采样策略；3）消融实验证实DPO目标和稀疏MoE模块对性能均有贡献；4）超参数分析显示K=50时达到最佳平衡；5）推理延迟与基线几乎相同，训练开销可忽略。

Conclusion: RoDPO通过随机Top-K采样策略有效解决了DPO在推荐系统中的假阴性困境，实现了偏好对齐与模型容量的平衡。该方法在保持推理效率的同时显著提升了多模态序列推荐的性能，为将偏好优化技术应用于推荐系统提供了稳健的方法论。未来工作可探索在其他多模态领域的泛化性和大规模基础模型的高效适配。

Abstract: Preference-based alignment objectives have been widely adopted, from RLHF-style pairwise learning in large language models to emerging applications in recommender systems. Yet, existing work rarely examines how Direct Preference Optimization (DPO) behaves under implicit feedback, where unobserved items are not reliable negatives. We conduct systematic experiments on multimodal sequential recommendation to compare common negative-selection strategies and their interaction with DPO training. Our central finding is that a simple modification, replacing deterministic hard negatives with stochastic sampling from a dynamic top-K candidate pool, consistently improves ranking performance. We attribute its effectiveness to two factors: (1) reducing erroneous suppressive gradients caused by false negatives, and (2) retaining informative hard signals while smoothing optimization via controlled stochasticity. With an optional sparse Mixture-of-Experts encoder for efficient capacity scaling, RoDPO achieves up to 5.25% NDCG@5 on three Amazon benchmarks, with nearly unchanged inference cost.

### [3] [Drift-Aware Continual Tokenization for Generative Recommendation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.29705)
*Yuebo Feng,Jiahao Liu,Mingzhe Han,Dongsheng Li,Hansu Gu,Peng Zhang,Tun Lu,Ning Gu*

Main category: cs.IR

TL;DR: DACT是一个用于生成式推荐的漂移感知持续标记化框架，通过分离"应变化"与"应稳定"的项表示，结合协作漂移识别模块和分层代码重分配策略，在持续更新标记器的同时保持先前学习的标记-嵌入对齐，有效平衡可塑性与稳定性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 生成式推荐系统采用两阶段流程：可学习标记器将项目映射为离散标记序列，生成式推荐模型基于这些标识符进行预测。现有协作感知标记器将协作信号纳入标记器，但当新项目和新交互持续到达时面临两个挑战：1）新项目导致标识符冲突和偏移；2）新交互反映项目流行度和共现模式的变化，导致协作漂移。完全重新训练标记器和GRM计算成本过高，而简单微调标记器会改变大多数现有项目的标记序列，破坏GRM学习的标记-嵌入对齐。需要一种平衡可塑性与稳定的持续标记化解决方案。

Method: DACT采用两阶段框架：第一阶段通过协作漂移识别模块（CDIM）与标记器端到端联合训练，输出项目级漂移置信度，实现漂移项和稳定项的差异化优化；第二阶段采用从宽松到严格的分层代码重分配策略，基于微调后的潜在表示更新标记序列。CDIM学习可重用的漂移模式和更新策略原型，通过注意力机制计算漂移置信度，将项目分为漂移集和稳定集，分别应用不同的优化目标。分层重分配策略在第一层总是重新分配代码以反映显著漂移，在深层仅当第一层代码变化时才触发重分配，最大限度减少不必要的标记变化。

Result: 在三个真实世界数据集（Beauty、Tools、Toys）和两个代表性GRM骨干（TIGER和LC-Rec）上的实验表明，DACT在多个评估设置下始终优于强基线方法。消融研究验证了各组件（CDIM、差异化更新策略、全局稳定性约束、代码重分配策略）的必要性。DACT显著降低了代码变更率（相比全微调），在保持标记-嵌入对齐的同时有效适应协作漂移。参数敏感性分析确定了最佳超参数配置，训练效率优于联合微调策略。

Conclusion: DACT成功解决了生成式推荐中协作感知标记器的持续学习问题，通过漂移感知的选择性适应策略，在适应新交互的同时保留先验知识。该框架有效平衡了可塑性与稳定性，为实际部署中的协作漂移提供了实用的持续标记化解决方案。未来工作可探索在解耦协作信号下的替代持续学习策略，以及大规模项目语料库中分层重分配规则的变化。

Abstract: Generative recommendation commonly adopts a two-stage pipeline in which a learnable tokenizer maps items to discrete token sequences (i.e. identifiers) and an autoregressive generative recommender model (GRM) performs prediction based on these identifiers. Recent tokenizers further incorporate collaborative signals so that items with similar user-behavior patterns receive similar codes, substantially improving recommendation quality. However, real-world environments evolve continuously: new items cause identifier collision and shifts, while new interactions induce collaborative drift in existing items (e.g., changing co-occurrence patterns and popularity). Fully retraining both tokenizer and GRM is often prohibitively expensive, yet naively fine-tuning the tokenizer can alter token sequences for the majority of existing items, undermining the GRM's learned token-embedding alignment. To balance plasticity and stability for collaborative tokenizers, we propose DACT, a Drift-Aware Continual Tokenization framework with two stages: (i) tokenizer fine-tuning, augmented with a jointly trained Collaborative Drift Identification Module (CDIM) that outputs item-level drift confidence and enables differentiated optimization for drifting and stationary items; and (ii) hierarchical code reassignment using a relaxed-to-strict strategy to update token sequences while limiting unnecessary changes. Experiments on three real-world datasets with two representative GRMs show that DACT consistently achieves better performance than baselines, demonstrating effective adaptation to collaborative evolution with reduced disruption to prior knowledge. Our implementation is publicly available at https://github.com/HomesAmaranta/DACT for reproducibility.

### [4] [Cold-Starts in Generative Recommendation: A Reproducibility Study](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.29845)
*Zhen Zhang,Jujia Zhao,Xinyu Ma,Xin Xin,Maarten de Rijke,Zhaochun Ren*

Main category: cs.IR

TL;DR: 本文对生成式推荐系统在冷启动场景下的性能进行了系统性可复现研究，通过统一评估协议和分离关键设计维度，揭示了模型规模、标识符设计和训练策略对冷启动性能的影响。研究发现：冷启动问题具有高度不对称性（物品冷启动比用户冷启动更具挑战性）；模型规模扩大仅带来边际改进；文本标识符能显著改善物品冷启动但会损害热启动性能；强化学习在冷启动场景下并不总是有效。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 冷启动推荐是动态开放世界平台中的核心挑战，需要模型在稀疏或缺失交互信号的情况下为新用户推荐物品，或为新物品向现有用户推荐。尽管基于预训练语言模型的生成式推荐器被期望通过利用物品语义信息和测试时条件化来缓解冷启动问题，但现有研究很少将冷启动作为主要评估设置，且由于模型规模、标识符设计和训练策略等关键设计选择经常同时改变，报告的性能增益难以解释。这促使研究者进行系统性可复现研究，以分离和评估这些关键因素在统一冷启动协议下的影响。

Conclusion: 生成式推荐系统在冷启动场景下的性能受到多个设计因素的复杂影响。模型规模扩大仅提供有限增益，无法从根本上解决冷启动问题。标识符设计是关键因素，需要在语义泛化和标识符可区分性之间进行权衡。强化学习在冷启动分布偏移下可能不适用。研究为增强生成式推荐系统在冷启动场景下的泛化能力提供了实证证据和实践指导，强调需要针对冷启动特性进行专门的设计和优化。

Recent generative recommenders built on pre-trained language models (PLMs) are often expected to mitigate cold-start by using item semantic information (e.g., titles and descriptions) and test-time conditioning on limited user context. However, cold-start is rarely treated as a primary evaluation setting in existing studies, and reported gains are difficult to interpret because key design choices, such as model scale, identifier design, and training strategy, are frequently changed together. In this work, we present a systematic reproducibility study of generative recommendation under a unified suite of cold-start protocols.

Motivation: 多模态重排序面临的主要挑战是模态鸿沟问题：文本重排序器本质上更接近文本候选而非图像候选，导致跨模态排序存在偏差和次优结果。现有VLM-based重排序器通常将候选统一编码为图像格式，这种处理方式引入了大量计算开销。同时，现有的开源多模态重排序器通常在通用领域数据上训练，在特定领域场景下表现不佳。需要一种能够原生处理混合文本-图像候选集、避免模态转换开销、并支持领域自适应的重排序框架。

Method: UniRank采用两阶段训练框架：1）监督微调阶段：将重排序任务构建为指令跟随任务，通过序列化查询-候选对为聊天格式，训练VLM输出离散相关性标签，并从标签token似然性推导出统一标量分数；2）偏好对齐阶段：在保留数据上挖掘硬负样本（SFT模型高评分但不相关的候选），构建领域内成对偏好数据，训练奖励模型，并通过基于查询分组的GRPO强化学习算法进行策略优化。框架基于Qwen3-VL骨干模型，使用LoRA进行参数高效微调。

Result: 在科学文献检索和设计专利搜索两个领域特定任务上的实验表明，UniRank consistently outperforms state-of-the-art baseline methods。在科学文献检索任务上，Recall@1提升8.9%，在专利搜索任务上Recall@1提升5.5%。同时，UniRank避免了将文本转换为图像的开销，存储需求降低7.1倍（科学领域）和1.4倍（专利领域），推理延迟降低2.6-2.8倍。消融实验验证了各组件（SFT、硬负样本挖掘、RLHF）的贡献，且在不同第一阶段检索器和不同骨干模型规模下均保持稳健性能。

Conclusion: UniRank提供了一个端到端的框架，用于训练领域特定的重排序器处理混合文本-图像候选集。通过利用VLM的强跨模态对齐能力，实现了原生统一的相关性评分，避免了模态转换开销。两阶段训练流程（指令驱动的SFT和基于硬负样本的偏好对齐）有效提升了领域特定场景下的排序质量。实验证明该框架在准确性和效率方面均优于现有方法，为资源受限的混合候选集多模态检索系统提供了实用且可扩展的解决方案。

Abstract: Reranking is a critical component in many information retrieval pipelines. Despite remarkable progress in text-only settings, multimodal reranking remains challenging, particularly when the candidate set contains hybrid text and image items. A key difficulty is the modality gap: a text reranker is intrinsically closer to text candidates than to image candidates, leading to biased and suboptimal cross-modal ranking. Vision-language models (VLMs) mitigate this gap through strong cross-modal alignment and have recently been adopted to build multimodal rerankers. However, most VLM-based rerankers encode all candidates as images, and treating text as images introduces substantial computational overhead. Meanwhile, existing open-source multimodal rerankers are typically trained on general-domain data and often underperform in domain-specific scenarios. To address these limitations, we propose UniRank, a VLM-based reranking framework that natively scores and orders hybrid text-image candidates without any modality conversion. Building on this hybrid scoring interface, UniRank provides an end-to-end domain adaptation pipeline that includes: (1) an instruction-tuning stage that learns calibrated cross-modal relevance scoring by mapping label-token likelihoods to a unified scalar score; and (2) a hard-negative-driven preference alignment stage that constructs in-domain pairwise preferences and performs query-level policy optimization through reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF). Extensive experiments on scientific literature retrieval and design patent search demonstrate that UniRank consistently outperforms state-of-the-art baselines, improving Recall@1 by 8.9% and 7.3%, respectively.
