### [1] [Multi-Step Semantic Reasoning in Generative Retrieval](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12368)
*Steven Dong,Yubao Tang,Maarten de Rijke*

Main category: cs.IR

TL;DR: ReasonGR是一个增强生成式检索模型在数值上下文中多步语义推理能力的框架，通过结构化提示策略和推理适配模块，在金融QA任务上提高了检索准确性。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有生成式检索模型在需要复杂推理的数值上下文（如金融报告分析）中表现不佳，主要因为两个限制：1）模型将检索视为黑盒操作，缺乏显式、可解释的推理步骤；2）训练主要基于最大似然估计，没有明确培养多步逻辑推理能力。这导致在FinQA等需要复杂推理的金融查询任务中检索准确率较低。

Method: ReasonGR采用双管齐下的方法：1）结构化提示策略：结合任务特定指令和链式思维提示，引导模型进行逐步推理；2）推理适配模块：使用LoRA（低秩适配）和QLoRA（量化LoRA）技术，在冻结的主干模型权重上注入可训练的低秩矩阵，专门学习推理相关参数。训练时采用自定义损失函数，结合精确匹配、部分匹配、集合匹配和结构相似性等多种评估信号。

Conclusion: ReasonGR通过引导式提示和高效微调增强了生成式检索模型的推理能力。结合少样本和CoT提示以及LoRA和自适应损失，ReasonGR在复杂数值推理任务上提高了检索准确性。尽管受限于模型大小和数据集范围，这项工作展示了推理驱动生成式检索的潜力。未来工作将探索更大模型、更精细的损失设计和更广泛的评估基准。

This limitation leads to suboptimal retrieval accuracy and hinders practical applicability. We propose ReasonGR, a framework designed to enhance multi-step semantic reasoning in numerical contexts within GR. ReasonGR employs a structured prompting strategy combining task-specific instructions with stepwise reasoning guidance to better address complex retrieval queries. Additionally, it integrates a reasoning-focused adaptation module to improve the learning of reasoning-related parameters. Experiments on the FinQA dataset, which contains financial queries over complex documents, demonstrate that ReasonGR improves retrieval accuracy and consistency, indicating its potential for advancing GR models in reasoning-intensive retrieval scenarios.

Motivation: 多模态推荐通常被视为特征融合问题，但原始视觉特征主要保留外观相似性，而用户决策通常由更高层次的语义因素（如风格、材质、使用场景）驱动。现有方法主要关注如何融合异质模态，但忽略了模态空间本身是否适合偏好匹配。本文提出，多模态推荐不仅是一个融合问题，更是一个语义对齐问题，需要将多模态内容组织到与偏好匹配兼容的语义空间中。

Extensive experiments on multiple multimodal recommendation datasets show that VLM4Rec consistently improves performance over raw visual features and several fusion-based alternatives, suggesting that representation quality may matter more than fusion complexity in this setting. The code is released at https://github.com/tyvalencia/enhancing-mm-rec-sys.

### [3] [FGTR: Fine-Grained Multi-Table Retrieval via Hierarchical LLM Reasoning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12702)
*Chaojie Sun,Bin Cao,Tiantian Li,Chenyu Hou,Ruizhe Li,Qing Fan*

Main category: cs.IR

TL;DR: 本文提出了FGTR（Fine-Grained Multi-Table Retrieval），一种基于大语言模型的层次化多表查询方法。该方法模拟人类阅读表格的认知过程，通过先进行模式检索识别相关列，再进行单元格检索定位具体内容，最终构建与查询对齐的简洁子表。在两个新构建的基准数据集上，FGTR在F2指标上比现有最优方法提升了18%，同时显著减少了计算开销。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有基于大语言模型的表格检索方法存在两个主要问题：1）通常采用粗粒度编码整个表格进行相似度匹配，这会引入大量查询无关数据导致准确性低，且处理大表格时效率低下；2）主要关注单表查询，而实际应用中信息往往分布在多个表中需要通过表连接进行整合。此外，大语言模型在处理结构化表格数据时面临上下文窗口限制和难以理解表格结构关系的挑战。

Method: FGTR采用层次化推理策略，分为离线预处理和在线检索两个阶段。离线阶段包括：1）模式语义化，使用LLM为表格和列生成自然语言描述；2）连接路径发现，计算表间潜在外键关系；3）列式存储构建，将单元格编码到向量数据库。在线阶段采用"解析-映射-填充"策略：首先进行模式检索，通过问题解析提取关键元素，映射到具体模式，并填充连接键；然后进行单元格检索，通过范围解析确定值范围，使用自适应映射机制（语义相似度匹配和精确值匹配）定位单元格，最终构建子表。

Result: 实验结果表明：1）在SpiderQA和BirdQA数据集上，FGTR在模式检索和单元格检索方面均优于现有方法，F2指标显著提升；2）在GPT-4o支持下，FGTR在召回率和精确度之间实现了优越的平衡，召回率保持SOTA水平的同时显著提升了精确度；3）在下游任务评估中，FGTR在四个表格推理数据集上均取得了最高的准确率；4）FGTR对大规模表格表现出更好的鲁棒性，当表格规模超过10^7个token时，TableRAG性能急剧下降而FGTR保持稳定；5）通过知识蒸馏，Llama3-8B模型在Spider数据集上的召回率提升了7.77个百分点。

Conclusion: FGTR通过层次化推理策略有效解决了细粒度多表检索的挑战，将复杂任务分解为模式检索和单元格检索两个可管理的阶段，充分利用了大语言模型的推理能力。该方法在保持高召回率的同时显著提升了精确度，能够检索出更简洁相关的子表，为更准确高效的表格推理系统奠定了基础。

Abstract: With the rapid advancement of large language models (LLMs), growing efforts have been made on LLM-based table retrieval. However, existing studies typically focus on single-table query, and implement it by similarity matching after encoding the entire table. These methods usually result in low accuracy due to their coarse-grained encoding which incorporates much query-irrelated data, and are also inefficient when dealing with large tables, failing to fully utilize the reasoning capabilities of LLM. Further, multi-table query is under-explored in retrieval tasks. To this end, we propose a hierarchical multi-table query method based on LLM: Fine-Grained Multi-Table Retrieval FGTR, a new retrieval paradigm that employs a human-like reasoning strategy. Through hierarchical reasoning, FGTR first identifies relevant schema elements and then retrieves the corresponding cell contents, ultimately constructing a concise and accurate sub-table that aligns with the given query. To comprehensively evaluate the performance of FGTR, we construct two new benchmark datasets based on Spider and BIRD . Experimental results show that FGTR outperforms previous state-of-the-art methods, improving the F_2 metric by 18% on Spider and 21% on BIRD, demonstrating its effectiveness in enhancing fine-grained retrieval and its potential to improve end-to-end performance on table-based downstream tasks.

### [4] [Anchored Alignment: Preventing Positional Collapse in Multimodal Recommender Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.12726)
*Yonghun Jeong,David Yoon Suk Kang,Yeon-Chang Lee*

Main category: cs.IR

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种名为AMP（Anchor Modal Preserve）的多模态推荐系统框架，通过在轻量级投影域中进行基于锚点的间接对齐，解决了现有对齐方法中模态特定结构模糊和ID主导的问题，同时保持了跨模态一致性。

  <summary>Details</summary>

By decoupling alignment from representation learning, AnchorRec preserves each modality's native structure while maintaining cross modal consistency and avoiding positional collapse. Experiments on four Amazon datasets show that AnchorRec achieves competitive top N recommendation accuracy, while qualitative analyses demonstrate improved multimodal expressiveness and coherence. The codebase of AnchorRec is available at https://github.com/hun9008/AnchorRec.
