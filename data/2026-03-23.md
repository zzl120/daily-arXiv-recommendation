### [1] [Spectral Tempering for Embedding Compression in Dense Passage Retrieval](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.19339)
*Yongkang Li,Panagiotis Eustratiadis,Evangelos Kanoulas*

Main category: cs.IR

TL;DR: 本文提出了Spectral Tempering (SpecTemp)，一种无需学习的后处理压缩方法，用于稠密检索嵌入的降维。该方法通过分析语料库特征谱的局部信噪比(SNR)，自适应地推导出维度相关的温度指数β，从而在方差保持和等方性之间取得平衡，无需任务特定的调优或标记数据。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 稠密检索系统在大规模部署时面临高维嵌入带来的存储和计算成本问题。现有的后处理降维方法存在根本性权衡：主成分分析(PCA)保留主要方差但未充分利用表示能力，而白化(Whitening)强制等方性但会放大重尾特征谱中的噪声。中间的光谱缩放方法使用固定超参数β，需要任务特定的调优，且忽略了最优β随目标维度k变化的系统性规律。

Method: SpecTemp方法包含三个阶段：1) 光谱分解：计算语料库嵌入的协方差矩阵并进行特征分解；2) SNR引导的指数推导：通过局部SNR分析估计噪声基底，使用Kneedle算法检测SNR曲线的拐点，基于子空间SNR自适应计算温度指数β；3) 嵌入变换：使用推导出的β构建变换矩阵，将原始嵌入压缩到目标维度k。该方法完全无需学习，仅需一次语料库特征分解。

Result: 在六个不同的稠密检索模型(Qwen3-8B、Jina-v4、GTE-7B等)和四个检索数据集(MS MARCO、Natural Questions、FEVER、FiQA)上的实验表明，SpecTemp在大多数配置中达到最佳或并列最佳性能。与网格搜索得到的最优β*相比，SpecTemp实现了接近oracle的性能，平均nDCG@10差异仅为0.05点。该方法在不同模型架构和尺度上表现出鲁棒性，自动适应不同压缩程度的需求。

Conclusion: Spectral Tempering通过从特征谱的局部SNR剖面自适应推导维度相关的温度指数β，有效解决了稠密检索嵌入后处理压缩中的方差保持与等方性权衡问题。该方法无需超参数调优、标记数据或模型重训练，在各种模型和数据集上一致达到接近最优的性能，为无需学习的嵌入压缩提供了一个实用的基准方法。

Abstract: Dimensionality reduction is critical for deploying dense retrieval systems at scale, yet mainstream post-hoc methods face a fundamental trade-off: principal component analysis (PCA) preserves dominant variance but underutilizes representational capacity, while whitening enforces isotropy at the cost of amplifying noise in the heavy-tailed eigenspectrum of retrieval embeddings. Intermediate spectral scaling methods unify these extremes by reweighting dimensions with a power coefficient $γ$, but treat $γ$ as a fixed hyperparameter that requires task-specific tuning. We show that the optimal scaling strength $γ$ is not a global constant: it varies systematically with target dimensionality $k$ and is governed by the signal-to-noise ratio (SNR) of the retained subspace. Based on this insight, we propose Spectral Tempering (\textbf{SpecTemp}), a learning-free method that derives an adaptive $γ(k)$ directly from the corpus eigenspectrum using local SNR analysis and knee-point normalization, requiring no labeled data or validation-based search. Extensive experiments demonstrate that Spectral Tempering consistently achieves near-oracle performance relative to grid-searched $γ^*(k)$ while remaining fully learning-free and model-agnostic. Our code is publicly available at https://anonymous.4open.science/r/SpecTemp-0D37.

### [2] [From Token to Item: Enhancing Large Language Models for Recommendation via Item-aware Attention Mechanism](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.19693)
*Xiaokun Zhang,Bowei He,Jiamin Chen,Ziqiang Cui,Chen Ma*

Main category: cs.IR

TL;DR: 本文提出了一种名为IAM（Item-aware Attention Mechanism）的新框架，通过引入项目感知注意力机制来增强大型语言模型在推荐任务中的表现。该方法将令牌关系分为项目内和项目间两种类型，分别通过专门的注意力层处理，从而在保持项目作为独立单元的同时有效捕获项目级别的协同关系。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有基于LLM的推荐方法通常将项目表示为令牌序列，并应用标准的注意力机制在这些令牌上生成推荐。然而，这种令牌中心的方法忽视了项目作为推荐基本单元的重要性，无法有效捕获项目级别的协同关系。作者发现现有方法存在关键限制：它们主要关注令牌-令牌关系，而忽略了在项目级别有效捕获协同信息的能力。

Method: 提出IAM框架，包含两个互补的注意力层：1）项目内注意力层：限制注意力在同一项目内的令牌之间，建模项目内容语义；2）项目间注意力层：专门关注跨项目的令牌关系，捕获项目协同关系。这两个层按顺序堆叠q次（q与LLM骨干网络的深度对齐），形成完整的项目感知注意力机制。使用基于分数的预测范式，通过推荐适配器将LLM输出映射到项目得分向量。

Result: 在三个公开数据集（Grocery、Arts、Cellphones）上的实验表明，IAM在Precision@10和NDCG@10指标上平均提升34.54%和25.81%，显著优于所有基线方法。消融实验验证了项目内和项目间注意力层的有效性，以及它们堆叠顺序的重要性。不同LLM骨干网络深度的实验显示，随着网络深度增加，性能持续提升。

Conclusion: IAM通过区分项目内和项目间令牌关系，将项目巩固为独立单元，并显式利用项目级别的协同信息，有效解决了现有LLM推荐方法中令牌中心范式的问题。实验证明了该框架在增强LLM推荐能力方面的优越性。未来工作包括整合多模态信息、设计用户自适应的注意力模块以及开发轻量级变体以提高可扩展性。

Abstract: Large Language Models (LLMs) have recently gained increasing attention in the field of recommendation. Existing LLM-based methods typically represent items as token sequences, and apply attention layers on these tokens to generate recommendations. However, by inheriting the standard attention mechanism, these methods focus on modeling token-level relations. This token-centric focus overlooks the item as the fundamental unit of recommendation, preventing existing methods from effectively capturing collaborative relations at the item level. In this work, we revisit the role of tokens in LLM-driven recommendation and categorize their relations into two types: (1) intra-item token relations, which present the content semantics of an item, e.g., name, color, and size; and (2) inter-item token relations, which encode collaborative relations across items. Building on these insights, we propose a novel framework with an item-aware attention mechanism (IAM) to enhance LLMs for recommendation. Specifically, IAM devises two complementary attention layers: (1) an intra-item attention layer, which restricts attention to tokens within the same item, modeling item content semantics; and (2) an inter-item attention layer, which attends exclusively to token relations across items, capturing item collaborative relations. Through this stacked design, IAM explicitly emphasizes items as the fundamental units in recommendation, enabling LLMs to effectively exploit item-level collaborative relations. Extensive experiments on several public datasets demonstrate the effectiveness of IAM in enhancing LLMs for personalized recommendation.

### [4] [How Well Does Generative Recommendation Generalize?](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.19809)

Motivation: 大型制造企业面临数据孤岛问题，不同部门维护的独立数据库导致信息检索困难。在航空航天领域，电子元件资格认证数据分散在两个异构数据库中：PLM-DB（产品生命周期管理数据库）和QC（资格认证数据库）。设计师需要交叉查询这两个数据库来确定元件的资格状态，但数据不一致、命名不规范以及非结构化文本内容使得这一过程耗时且容易出错。资格认证过程成本高昂，重用现有资格认证对优化新资格规划至关重要。

Method: 采用三阶段混合方法：1) 使用GPT-OSS-120B LLM进行半自动化数据清洗，包括制造商名称规范化和从非结构化文本中提取零件号；2) 基于Ontop框架构建虚拟知识图谱，定义语义模型和映射关系，通过Ontop Lens处理数据异构性；3) 实现双重检索机制：使用SPARQL查询检索直接资格认证和相似资格认证，使用BGE嵌入模型进行向量化搜索检索替代资格认证。同时与基于RAG的方法进行对比分析。

Result: 系统成功部署在Thales Alenia Space生产环境中，支持约50名用户。在675个测试组件中，VKG+LLM方法在直接资格认证、相似资格认证和替代资格认证的准确率分别为86.03%、90.42%和36.87%。成本效益分析显示，虽然VKG+LLM方法需要约60人天的初始设置时间，但每个组件的平均处理时间仅为5分钟，相比手动方法（40分钟）和RAG方法（15分钟）效率显著提升。在组件数量超过一定阈值后，VKG+LLM方法在长期效率上优于纯LLM方法。

Conclusion: 提出的VKG+LLM混合管道有效解决了航空航天领域电子元件资格认证数据集成和检索的挑战。该方法结合了语义技术的精确性和LLM的灵活性，在保证准确性的同时提高了检索效率。相比纯RAG方法，该方案更适合长期、大规模的关键领域应用。未来工作将探索LLM在知识工程任务（如本体设计和映射生成）中的应用，以进一步降低初始设置成本。

Abstract: Large manufacturing companies face challenges in information retrieval due to data silos maintained by different departments, leading to inconsistencies and misalignment across databases. This paper presents an experience in integrating and retrieving qualification data for electronic components used in satellite board design. Due to data silos, designers cannot immediately determine the qualification status of individual components. However, this process is critical during the planning phase, when assembly drawings are issued before production, to optimize new qualifications and avoid redundant efforts. To address this, we propose a pipeline that uses Virtual Knowledge Graphs for a unified view over heterogeneous data sources and LLMs to enhance retrieval and reduce manual effort in data cleansing. The retrieval of qualifications is then performed through an Ontology-based Data Access approach for structured queries and a vector search mechanism for retrieving qualifications based on similar textual properties. We perform a comparative cost-benefit analysis, demonstrating that the proposed pipeline also outperforms approaches relying solely on LLMs, such as Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), in terms of long-term efficiency.
